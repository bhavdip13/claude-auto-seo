# SEO Guidelines

> These are the SEO rules Claude Auto SEO applies to all content.
> Customize these to match your site's standards.

---

## Content Length Requirements

| Content Type | Minimum | Target | Maximum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blog posts (informational) | 1,500 words | 2,500–3,500 words | 5,000 words |
| How-to guides | 2,000 words | 3,000–4,000 words | 6,000 words |
| Landing pages | 800 words | 1,200–2,000 words | 3,000 words |
| Product pages | 400 words | 600–1,000 words | 1,500 words |
| News/updates | 300 words | 500–800 words | 1,200 words |

---

## Keyword Optimization Rules

- **Primary keyword density:** 1.0% – 2.0% (never exceed 3%)
- **Primary keyword in:** H1, first 100 words, 2+ H2s, conclusion
- **Secondary keywords:** 3–5 per article, naturally integrated
- **LSI/semantic keywords:** 5–10 per article
- **Anchor text:** Descriptive, never exact-match only

---

## Meta Tag Standards

### Title Tags
- Length: **50–60 characters** (never over 60)
- Primary keyword: within first 30 characters when possible
- Brand name: append at end if space allows
- Format: `[Primary Keyword] — [Benefit/Hook] | [Brand]`

### Meta Descriptions
- Length: **150–160 characters**
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Include a call to action or benefit statement
- Never duplicate across pages

---

## Heading Structure

```
H1 — One per page only. Full target keyword. Compelling.
  H2 — Major sections. Include keyword variations.
    H3 — Subsections within H2. Supporting topics.
      H4 — Only when content genuinely needs 4 levels.
```

- Subheading frequency: Every **300–400 words**
- H2s should form a complete outline of the topic

---

## Link Guidelines

### Internal Links
- Minimum per article: **3 links**
- Maximum per article: **7 links**
- Anchor text: Descriptive (not "click here" or "read more")
- Priority pages to link: Pillar content, product pages, high-converting pages

### External Links
- Minimum per article: **2 links**
- Maximum per article: **4 links**
- Only link to: Authority sites (.gov, .edu, major publications, research)
- Open in new tab: Yes (`target="_blank" rel="noopener"`)
- Never link to: Competitors, spammy sites, irrelevant sources

---

## Readability Standards

- **Reading level:** Grade 8–10 (Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Flesch Reading Ease:** 50–70
- **Average sentence length:** 15–20 words
- **Passive voice:** Under 15% of sentences
- **Paragraph length:** 2–4 sentences
- **Transition words:** In 30%+ of sentences

---

## E-E-A-T Requirements

Every article should include at least:
- [ ] Author byline with credentials or bio
- [ ] At least 2 external citations to authoritative sources
- [ ] Publication date and "last updated" date
- [ ] Original perspective or first-hand experience signal
- [ ] Contact info or about page accessible from content

---

## Schema Markup Defaults

Apply automatically to:
- **All blog posts:** `Article` or `BlogPosting`
- **How-to content:** `HowTo` with steps
- **FAQ sections:** `FAQPage` (note: restricted for non-health/gov sites as of Aug 2023 — use only for high-quality, genuinely useful FAQs)
- **Product/service pages:** `Product` or `Service`
- **Author pages:** `Person`
- **Homepage:** `Organization` + `WebSite` with `SearchAction`
- **All pages:** `BreadcrumbList`

---

## Content Quality Checklist

Before any article is published, verify:

### Content
- [ ] Answers the primary search query in full
- [ ] Provides unique value not found in top 10 results
- [ ] All statistics are current (within 2 years) and cited
- [ ] Includes at least 1 real example or case study
- [ ] Has a clear conclusion with next step / CTA

### Technical
- [ ] No duplicate content vs existing pages
- [ ] Images compressed and have alt text
- [ ] All internal links working
- [ ] No broken external links
- [ ] Canonical tag set correctly

### SEO
- [ ] Primary keyword density 1–2%
- [ ] Keyword in H1, first 100 words, 2+ H2s
- [ ] Title tag 50–60 characters
- [ ] Meta description 150–160 characters
- [ ] 3–5 internal links
- [ ] 2–3 external links

---

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Keyword stuffing (>3% density)
- ❌ Duplicate meta tags across pages
- ❌ Thin content under 800 words
- ❌ Auto-generated content without human review
- ❌ Doorway pages (pages only for rankings, not users)
- ❌ Hidden text or cloaking
- ❌ Paid link schemes or link farms
- ❌ Automated profile submissions or bookmarking bots
