# CRO Best Practices

> Conversion rate optimization guidelines for landing pages, blog CTAs, and forms.
> Claude Auto SEO uses these when writing landing pages and analyzing content.

---

## Above-the-Fold Rules

Every page's "above the fold" (visible without scrolling) must contain:
1. **H1 headline** — clear benefit, not clever wordplay
2. **Supporting subheadline** — expands on H1 with specific detail
3. **Primary CTA button** — visible, contrasting color, specific copy
4. **At least one trust signal** — logo bar, review count, or guarantee

**5-Second Test:** A new visitor should understand:
- What is this? ✓
- Who is it for? ✓
- Why should I care? ✓
- What do I do next? ✓

---

## CTA Copy Formula

❌ Weak: "Submit", "Click Here", "Learn More", "Read More"
✅ Strong: "[Verb] [Specific Outcome]"

Examples:
- "Start My Free 14-Day Trial"
- "Get My SEO Audit (Free)"
- "Download the Complete Guide"
- "Show Me How It Works"
- "Book a Free Strategy Call"

**Rule:** The CTA should complete the sentence "I want to ___"

---

## Social Proof Best Practices

### Testimonials
- Include: full name, photo, job title, company
- Make it specific: "increased traffic by 340%" beats "great tool"
- Place near CTA or at the point of hesitation

### Numbers
- Show specific metrics: "14,000+ customers" not "thousands"
- Star ratings: show the number of reviews, not just stars
- Years in business, awards, certifications

### Logo Bars
- 6-8 recognizable logos only
- "Trusted by teams at..." headline
- Place above fold or just after hero section

## Form Optimization

| Field Count | Best For |
|---|---|
| 1 field (email only) | Newsletter signup |
| 2-3 fields | Free tool / lead magnet |
| 4-5 fields | Free trial / demo |
| 6+ fields | Enterprise / high-intent |

**Rules:**
- Labels above fields (not inside as placeholder text)
- Submit button: descriptive copy, not "Submit"
- Show what happens next: "You'll get an email within 2 minutes"
- Reduce required fields — every extra field loses 10-15% of completions

---

## Trust Signals by Page Type

### SaaS / Software
- Free trial (no credit card required)
- Money-back guarantee
- Data security certifications (SOC 2, GDPR)
- Customer logos
- G2 / Capterra rating badge

### eCommerce
- Secure checkout badge
- Free returns / shipping policy
- Customer reviews with photos
- "As seen in" media logos
- Stock/scarcity indicators (authentic only)

### Service Business / Agency
- Case studies with specific results
- Client logos
- Certifications / awards
- Team photos and bios
- Years in business

---

## Urgency & Scarcity (Use Ethically)

✅ Authentic urgency:
- "Offer ends [specific date]"
- "Only [n] spots left this month"
- "Price increases on [date]"

❌ Fake urgency (destroys trust):
- Countdown timers that reset
- "Only 3 left!" when you have 3,000
- Perpetual "limited time" offers

---

## Mobile CRO Rules

- CTA buttons: minimum 48px tap target
- Font size: minimum 16px for body text
- No horizontal scrolling
- Sticky CTA bar at bottom on mobile
- Forms: trigger correct keyboard (email field shows email keyboard)
- Pop-ups: must have an obvious close button, not full-screen

---

## Page Speed & CRO

Every 1 second of load time = ~7% conversion drop.

Priorities:
1. LCP < 2.5s (main content loads fast)
2. No layout shift (CLS < 0.1) — buttons don't jump around
3. Images optimized (WebP, proper sizing)
4. No render-blocking scripts above fold
